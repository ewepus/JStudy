Volcano Erupts in Indonesia After Earthquake

In Indonesia on an island Sulawesi volcano Soputan awakens, releasing a column of ash about four meters high, Novye Izvestia writes. The volcano's eruption began this morning. Local residents were advised to stay away from him and use respirators.

The tremors caused a tsunami, the height of the waves reached 7 m. According to the latest data, more than 1,400 people died as a result of the natural disasters. The UN allocated $15 million to provide assistance to victims of the earthquake in Indonesia.
